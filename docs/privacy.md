# Privacy Policy

Calodoro is a macOS menu-bar app that helps track focus time and syncs it to Google Calendar when you sign in.

## Data we access
- Google Calendar events (read) to let you select existing all-day events
- Google Calendar events (write) to create or update events based on your focus time

## Data we store
- OAuth tokens are stored locally on your Mac
- App preferences are stored locally on your Mac

## Data we do not collect
- We do not run a server
- We do not collect or sell personal data
- We do not share your data with third parties

## Contact
If you have any questions about this policy, contact the app owner.
